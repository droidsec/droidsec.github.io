droidsec was founded in May 2012 in order to bring together the top Android-focused researchers in the community. Group members include security researchers from many different companies in the mobile and security spaces. You can find most of the members on the #droidsec channel on the Freenode IRC network.

Our goal is to advance the security of the Android platform and its surrounding ecosystem. This web site serves to collect security relevant information published all across the Internet into one convienient location. We hope you find the site useful. If you do, please consider donating.

Join Us!

The group is currently open for participation from anyone interested. In particular, we are seeking motivated individuals to help push the site forward.

  • Web Designers Help us make the site look and feel better to visitors.
  • Security Researchers Execute on projects and showcase them on the site!
